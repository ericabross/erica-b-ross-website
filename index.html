About Me

I’m Erica, and I design and build user-friendly, mobile-responsive websites. I focus on web design and front-end web development. I have brought skills from my marketing and education backgrounds to my work in tech. In addition to practicing the fundamentals of web design and development, I am experienced in WordPress development, UX design, branding design, and visual design.


Although I was born and raised in Louisiana, I spend much of my time avoiding seafood. When I am not behind the laptop, you can find me cuddling a dog, sipping a cocktail, watching reality TV, listening to pop culture and true crime podcasts, or traveling around the world at EPCOT.

From research to wireframing and prototyping, I create a seamless and cohesive user experience that eliminates frustration and maximizes usability.

I strive to design sites that are user-friendly and visually appealing. I also produce a variety of branding materials.

My HTML coding is organized and easy to read, which is ideal for collaboration and troubleshooting.

Just like my HTML, my CSS is clean and organized. I can easily transform a designer’s PSD into a live, interactive web page.

Using mobile-first design, I create sites that are compatible with mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.

Whether it is a blog or an online store, I design and set up sites that can be easily updated by my clients.
